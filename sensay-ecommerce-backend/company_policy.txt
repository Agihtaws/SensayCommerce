# Employee Handbook - Customer Service Guidelines

## Customer Service Standards
- Response time: Within 24 hours for email inquiries
- Phone support: Available Monday-Friday 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- Live chat: Available during business hours

## Return Policy
- 30-day return window for all products
- Items must be in original condition
- Free return shipping for defective items
- Refund processing time: 5-7 business days

## Product Information Guidelines
- All product descriptions must include specifications
- Images should be high-resolution (minimum 1200x1200px)
- Pricing must be updated within 24 hours of changes
- Stock levels updated in real-time

## Escalation Procedures
- Level 1: Customer service representative
- Level 2: Team supervisor
- Level 3: Department manager
- Level 4: Director of customer experience
